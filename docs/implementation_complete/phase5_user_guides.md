## Login Guide

### How to Sign In

1. **Navigate to the Login Page**
   - Go to `https://video-research-40c4b.firebaseapp.com/login`
   - Or click "Sign In" from the home page

2. **Sign In with Google**
   - Click the "Sign in with Google" button
   - A popup window will appear
   - Select your Google account or enter your Google credentials
   - Grant permissions if prompted

3. **First-Time Users**
   - New users are automatically created when signing in for the first time
   - You'll start with the Free tier (40 credits daily)
   - Credits reset every day at midnight UTC

4. **Returning Users**
   - Simply click "Sign in with Google" again
   - You'll be automatically logged in if your session is still valid

### Troubleshooting Login Issues

**Problem: Popup is blocked**
- Solution: Allow popups for this site in your browser settings

**Problem: "Invalid or expired token" error**
- Solution: Sign out and sign in again

**Problem: Can't select Google account**
- Solution: Clear browser cookies and try again, or use incognito/private mode

## Credit System Guide

### Understanding Credits

Credits are used to create video summaries. Each summary costs credits based on the number of videos:

- **1 video:** 20 credits
- **2 videos:** 30 credits
- **3 videos:** 40 credits
- **4-5 videos:** 60 credits
- **6-10 videos:** 120 credits

### Credit Balance

Your credit balance is displayed in the top-right corner of the application. It shows:
- Current balance
- Your tier (Free, Starter, Pro, or Premium)

### Credit Reset Schedule

**Free Tier:**
- Credits reset **daily** at midnight UTC
- Reset amount: **40 credits**

**Starter, Pro, and Premium Tiers:**
- Credits reset **monthly** on the first day of the month at midnight UTC
- Reset amounts:
  - Starter: **500 credits**
  - Pro: **2,000 credits**
  - Premium: **5,000 credits**

### Checking Your Credit Balance

1. Look at the top-right corner of the application
2. Your balance is displayed next to your profile/account icon
3. Click on it to see detailed credit information

### Insufficient Credits

If you don't have enough credits to create a summary:

1. You'll see an error message: "Insufficient credits"
2. The error will show:
   - Required credits for your batch
   - Your current balance
3. Options:
   - Wait for credit reset (daily for Free tier, monthly for premium tiers)
   - Request a tier upgrade (see [Tier Upgrade Request Guide](#tier-upgrade-request-guide))

### Credit Transactions

All credit changes are tracked. You can view your credit history in your account settings (if available).

## Tier Upgrade Request Guide

### Available Tiers

1. **Free Tier** (Default)
   - 40 credits per day
   - Daily reset
   - Maximum 3 videos per batch

2. **Starter Tier**
   - 500 credits per month
   - Monthly reset
   - Maximum 5 videos per batch

3. **Pro Tier**
   - 2,000 credits per month
   - Monthly reset
   - Maximum 8 videos per batch

4. **Premium Tier**
   - 5,000 credits per month
   - Monthly reset
   - Maximum 10 videos per batch

### How to Request a Tier Upgrade

1. **Navigate to Tier Upgrade Page**
   - Click "Upgrade Tier" or "Request Tier Upgrade" link
   - Or go to your account settings

2. **Select Desired Tier**
   - Review available tiers and their benefits
   - Click "Request Tier Upgrade" for your desired tier

3. **Submit Request**
   - Your request will be sent to the administrator
   - You'll receive a confirmation message
   - The administrator will review your request

4. **Contact Administrator**
   - Email: **cazevfung@gmail.com**
   - Include your email address and requested tier in your message
   - The administrator will process your request manually

### After Request Submission

- Your request status will be "Pending"
- The administrator will review your request
- You'll be notified when your request is approved or rejected
- If approved, your tier will be upgraded and credits will be reset

### Tier Upgrade Request Status

- **Pending:** Request submitted, awaiting review
- **Approved:** Tier upgraded, credits reset
- **Rejected:** Request denied (contact administrator for details)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Issue: "Insufficient credits" error

**Symptoms:**
- Error message when trying to create a summary
- Balance shows 0 or very low credits

**Solutions:**
1. Wait for credit reset (daily for Free tier, monthly for premium tiers)
2. Request a tier upgrade
3. Check your credit balance in account settings

#### Issue: Summary creation fails

**Symptoms:**
- Error message during processing
- Summary not created

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify YouTube URLs are valid
3. Ensure you have sufficient credits
4. Try again after a few minutes
5. Contact support if issue persists

#### Issue: Credits not resetting

**Symptoms:**
- Credits didn't reset at expected time
- Balance remains the same

**Solutions:**
1. Check the reset schedule:
   - Free tier: Daily at midnight UTC
   - Premium tiers: Monthly on the 1st at midnight UTC
2. Wait a few hours (reset may be delayed)
3. Refresh the page
4. Contact support if credits don't reset after 24 hours

#### Issue: Can't access summaries

**Symptoms:**
- Summaries not appearing in history
- "Access denied" error

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you're logged in with the correct account
2. Check that summaries belong to your account
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try signing out and signing in again

#### Issue: Authentication errors

**Symptoms:**
- "Invalid or expired token" error
- Can't stay logged in

**Solutions:**
1. Sign out and sign in again
2. Clear browser cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Check that popups are allowed
5. Contact support if issue persists
