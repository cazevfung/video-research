**CRITICAL - MANDATORY FORMAT**: You MUST use numbered citations in the format [1], [2], [3], etc. throughout your ENTIRE report.

- ❌ Text-based citations like "（视频1，...）" or "(Video 1, ...)" or "视频1" or "Video 1"
- ❌ Parenthetical citations like "(source 1)" or "（来源1）"
- ❌ Any other citation format

- ✅ Numbered citations in square brackets: [1], [2], [3], etc.
